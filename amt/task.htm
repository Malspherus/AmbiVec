Your job
You are shown some statements in the general area of movies (containing two entities and their connection) that you need to grade. You should ignore grammatical mistakes in the statements and only grade the entities and their connection. It doesn't matter if the statement is just one of multiple correct answers, just grade the statement you see. You will have to follow the provided links or use your preferred web search to get more information about the meaning of some entities as only their name is shown, but most entities appear in multiple statements. The rating you give a statement should depend on how close to the correct answer (which you might have to find yourself) the statement is, not just whether it is correct or not.

The code can be built by using the provided numbers (eg. x-y-z) and writing the x-th letter of the first entity, the y-th letter of the connection and the z-th letter of the second entity, small and capital letters can't be interchanged for this and spaces need to be counted as well and replaced with "_" in the code. This is not the only criteria for acceptance but a required one.

This example would be considered completely correct and rated with a rating of 5. Note that it wouldn't matter if there ware also other directors besides him or if he also had other relations to this movie.
